Kitengela's leading boys boarding school

Kitengela Boys High School is a private 8-4-4 curriculum boys school located 17 km from Kitengela town towards Isinya town opposite Kenchic farm along the Nairobi-Kitengela-Namanga road.

Open books, open minds, open doors

At Kitengela Boys High School, we deliver seeds of high quality education to all our students, Weaving bonds of belonging, empowering minds to solve for change, and fueling a lifelong thirst for knowledge, all watered with dedication.

We nurture the unique individuality, talent and self-assurance of each student, equipping them to take on the world with the skills and confidence to ensure success.

Your boy deserves only the best, given by the best.
Journey with the Kitengelians and you will know no regrets.
